.Conceptual assessment in the biological sciences - March 2007 meeting

Background:  To facilitate discuss and collaboration between groups working in the area of conceptual assessment in the biological sciences, we organized an NSF-funding meeting in March of 2007 on this topic.  Full meeting reports, and papers from individual attendees are available below, as pdfs. 

Meeting reports:

  • Gavin-Doxas, K., M.W. Klymkowsky & Susan Elrod. Building, Using, and Maximizing the Impact of Concept Inventories in the Biological Sciences. Life Science Education, submitted.
  • Stamp, TIEE meeting review, June 2007.
