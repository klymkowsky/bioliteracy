How does a scientist approach a problem/question?
(skills involved)

  • Does the question make sense? (analysis)
  • Is the question answerable? (technical savvy?)
  • If not, could it be answered if we knew more?
  • If so, what do we need to know (or assume)?
  • If a question is answerable without further information, are we asking students to answer it scientifically or are we asking students to remember the answer?
