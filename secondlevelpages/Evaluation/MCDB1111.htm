Example Questions for MCDB 1111 - UC Boulder


Question 1: Consider the different between natural selection, sexual selection, and the effects of small population size. Propose an experimental program to attempt to discern which was primarily responsible for a specific trait.

Question 2: You discover a new antibiotic that blocks the synthesis of ATP in aerobic bacteria. In the course of your studies, you find that cell treated with this antibiotic no longer untilize O2. Propose a hypothesis for how the antibiotic works and an experiment by which to test that hypothesis.

Question 3: You are studying a gene linked to premature aging.  You can find a transcript made from the gene, but no evidence that it encodes a polypeptide. What kind of evidence would suggest that the gene does not encode a polypeptide, and assuming that it does not, how might such a gene 'work'?
