Frequently asked questions

When will the BCI be ready?

We hope to have the first version of the Basic Biology Concept Inventory ready for testing by May of 2005.   We are tentatively planning a full "coming out" party for summer of 2006 at Denver University!

Will it cost money to use the BCI?

No, we will make if freely available.

Who are you guys, anyway?

The project was started by Kathy Garvin-Doxas and Mike Klymkowsky; but we have gotten help and support from lots of people. 

 
 

What can I do if I want to evaluate student learning before the BCI is ready, of if I teach subjects not covered in the Basic BCI?  

A simple, although more tedious method involves the use of "calibrated" essay questions that can be evaluated by various means.   We are using an automated essay evaluation system, involving Latent Semantic Analysis you may be able to use.  

If you are interested in collaborating with us, let us know by email.   

Visit our concept questions page, or start using the essay question site.
