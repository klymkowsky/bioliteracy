Examples of student responses
‘sexual selection’: According to Darwin survival of the fittest is a component of evolution.  Here sexual selection refers to individuals selecting a mate that possesses preferable genes; mating with such a mate would result in offspring with a higher chance of survival
“In molecular terms, describe how genetic informtion is stored and how it is used”
Genetic information is stored in DNA.  DNA is located in the nucleus of the cell.  Genetic information is the blueprint for everything in the organism.  It is translated and transcribed to create amino acids and proteins.
